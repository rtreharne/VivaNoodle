The challenge

Students still need to show they understand, even with AI in the mix.

Generative AI can churn out essays and code, but it can‚Äôt think on its feet. Universities need a fast, fair viva to prove students still own their ideas.

The risk

AI can polish or even write submissions, making it hard to tell who understands the work. A grade based on text alone can reward the tool, not the student.

Consequence: silent erosion of learning outcomes and grade integrity.

The need

Live questioning that probes decisions, evidence, and trade-offs. You need to see students think on their feet, not just turn in a perfected document.

Goal: verify ownership of ideas and surface reasoning, not just final wording.

The answer

Scaled vivas: guided, text-based dialogue that confirms comprehension and deters delegation to AI. Signals and transcripts keep outcomes, context, and integrity visible.

Result: authentic assessment at cohort scale without surveillance or scheduling chaos.
