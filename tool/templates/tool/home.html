The challenge

GenAI can write the work — it can’t defend it.

Use a viva to surface real reasoning, prove ownership, and keep grading fair without policing screens or banning tools.

The risk

AI can polish or even write submissions, making it hard to tell who understands the work. Grades based on text alone can reward the tool, not the student.

Consequence: silent erosion of learning outcomes and grade integrity.

The need

Live questioning that probes decisions, evidence, and trade-offs. You need to see students think on their feet, not just turn in a perfected document.

Goal: verify ownership of ideas and surface reasoning, not just final wording.

The answer

Scaled vivas: guided, text-based dialogue that confirms comprehension and deters delegation to AI. Signals and transcripts make authentic work visible.

Result: authentic assessment at cohort scale without surveillance or scheduling chaos.
